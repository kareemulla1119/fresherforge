The Importance of a Portfolio

In today’s competitive job market, having a portfolio isn’t just an option—it’s a necessity. A portfolio showcases your skills, achievements, and projects in a visually appealing format. It gives recruiters tangible proof of your capabilities.

  • Fact: 74% of recruiters prefer candidates who provide examples of their work.
  • Impact: Portfolios can increase your interview chances by 50%.
  • Advantage: Having a portfolio can lead to a 30% higher chance of being hired over candidates without one.
  • Downside: Without a portfolio, you may miss out on demonstrating your practical skills and uniqueness.

ATS-Friendly Resumes: Why They Matter

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software used by companies to filter resumes. A poorly formatted resume might never reach human eyes, regardless of your qualifications.

  • Fact: Over 75% of resumes are rejected by ATS systems before reaching recruiters.
  • Solution: ATS-friendly resumes use proper formatting, keywords, and simple designs to pass the initial screening.
  • Advantage: Ensuring your resume is ATS-compatible can increase your chances of getting an interview by up to 40%.
  • Downside: If your resume isn't ATS-friendly, it could be automatically rejected, regardless of your qualifications.
